Wiscansin Fest

These are from 2023. I was lucky enough to get the chance to take photos at WISCANSIN FEST in 2023. Illinois State Hockey

These photos are from my internship with Illinois State Hockey. I got to be in the bench taking videos and pictures for the players on the D1, D2, and D3 team. This was a great experience and I got to learn a lot more about my camera and how to better operate it for sports photography
